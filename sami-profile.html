Saami Salami Asl

Founder of Elisence — Global Health Intelligence Ecosystem

Short Profile

Saami Salami Asl is the founder and architect of Elisence, a multi-phase global health intelligence platform designed for families, clinics, and ministries.

His work focuses on safe, non-diagnostic digital health: Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1, Family Ecosystem 0–18, Nutrition, and Mental Health, all built on a Zero-Trust, privacy-first infrastructure.

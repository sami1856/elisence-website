1. A Global Problem: Health Systems Are Fragmented

Around the world, digital health is broken. Different apps, different systems, different hospitals — none of them talk to each other.

Families struggle with multiple apps. Clinicians face data overload. Ministries cannot see the national picture.

Elisence was created to fix this.

2. Designed From Day One With Zero-Trust

Most health platforms rely on “trusted internal networks.” Elisence does not.

Every request, every action, every feature must prove:

  • Who is asking?
  • What are they allowed to see?
  • What is the minimum data required?

This is the only safe architecture for modern health systems.

Who Is Saami Salami Asl?

Saami Salami Asl — Founder of Elisence

1. Background and Systems Thinking

Saami Salami Asl studied Electrical Engineering before later moving into Law. The combination of systems thinking, structured problem-solving, legal understanding, and human responsibility helped shape the long-term philosophy behind Elisence.

2. Human Experience and Real-World Perspective

Experience across care environments, vulnerable populations, family situations, and real-world systems contributed to a deeper understanding of the challenges people, carers, and healthcare systems face every day.

These experiences reinforced a belief that health technology should feel clearer, calmer, more supportive, and easier to navigate.

3. Founding Elisence

Saami Salami Asl founded Elisence as a global health platform focused on prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, privacy, safety, and human-centred digital health.

  • Trusted health intelligence
  • Privacy and Zero-Trust foundations
  • Human-centred digital health experiences
  • Multi-language accessibility
  • Continuity of care and prevention

Elisence is designed to help healthcare feel more connected, preventative, transparent, and supportive while respecting clear safety and responsibility boundaries.

4. A Human-Centred Health Philosophy

The founder philosophy behind Elisence is built around a simple idea: technology should support people, not overwhelm them.

  • Human dignity first
  • Transparent and explainable health intelligence
  • Safer, calmer health experiences
  • Respect for privacy and human wellbeing
  • Inclusive, culturally aware design

Elisence reflects a belief that health technology should strengthen trust, understanding, and continuity rather than create confusion.

5. Today and the Long-Term Vision

Today, Saami Salami Asl continues building Elisence as a long-term global health platform intended to support people, families, clinicians, and healthcare systems through prevention, continuity of care, trusted health intelligence, and more human-centred digital health experiences.

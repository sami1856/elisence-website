1. Why Nations Can No Longer Depend on Foreign Health AI

Health data is the most sensitive category in the world — more sensitive than finance, energy or defense.

  • data ownership
  • policy independence
  • algorithmic sovereignty

2. What “Sovereign Health AI” Actually Means

Sovereign Health AI ensures that a country controls:

  • where its data lives
  • who accesses it
  • how decisions are made

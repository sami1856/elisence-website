Elisence Family Ecosystem (0–18)

A safe, educational intelligence layer connecting growth, sleep, school signals, digital safety, emotional well-being, and family bonding.

Educational Only — Not Diagnostic

This module is designed to support families with clearer understanding. It does not diagnose, predict illness, or replace paediatric or emergency care.

1. Families Need a Joined-Up View

Families often juggle multiple systems: school portals, sleep trackers, growth charts, digital platforms, and emotional check-in tools.

The Elisence Family Ecosystem brings these signals together — in one safe, non-diagnostic, privacy-respecting layer.

2. What the Family Ecosystem Covers

  • Growth & basic health indicators (trend-based, not diagnostic)
  • Sleep patterns & daily routines
  • School & learning signals
  • Digital safety & screen balance
  • Emotional check-ins & stress patterns
  • Family bonding & shared moments

3. Digital Safety & Healthy Dopamine

  • Daily usage windows (morning, night, school hours)
  • Patterns showing imbalance between rest, school & screens
  • Educational content for healthier digital habits

4. Emotional & Mental Well-Being

For young people, emotions shift quickly. Elisence offers safe, age-appropriate reflection tools:

  • Mood & stress check-ins
  • Conversation starters for families
  • Guidance on when to consider professional support

5. Respecting Privacy & Autonomy

  • Minimised, purpose-limited data
  • Clear visibility for families
  • More autonomy for older teens
  • Aggregated, anonymised trends for schools & ministries

6. Population Insights — Without Exposing Children

Ministries and schools can use anonymised patterns — such as sleep stress or digital balance — to improve support systems without exposing individual children.

Official Founder Identity

Saami Salami Asl is the Founder and Architect of Elisence, a global health platform focused on prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, privacy, safety, and human-centred digital health.

Elisence is designed to help health feel more connected, preventative, supportive, trusted, and easier to navigate over time.

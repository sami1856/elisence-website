Supportive · Non-Judgmental · Dopamine-Positive · Audit-Ready

What Phase 3 Is

Phase 3 is the “care & coaching layer” of Elisence. While Phase 1 stores the data, and Phase 2 compares medications, Phase 3 is the part that interacts directly with the user’s daily life:

  • Daily check-ins and reflections
  • Weight-trend coaching (normal vs concerning changes)
  • Interpretation of appetite, sleep and energy patterns
  • GLP-1 schedule reminders (non-medical, supportive only)
  • Behavioural nudges designed around dopamine balance
  • Micro-rewards and motivational messaging
  • Context-aware insights powered by Elisa

How It Helps Users

Most weight-loss journeys fail because users feel lost or unsupported. Phase 3 fixes this by giving every person a personalised, human-like companion:

  • “Today your weight is stable — this is completely normal.”
  • “You slept poorly last night; this may increase appetite today.”
  • “You walked 2,400 steps more than yesterday — great job!”
  • “Side effects have decreased compared to last week.”

Elisa never judges, never pressures, and never uses fear. She supports, encourages, and explains — like a patient friend who understands health science deeply.

Dopamine-Positive Behaviour Design

Phase 3 is built on a behavioural model that focuses on **healthy dopamine stimulation**:

  • Micro-rewards (stars, gentle animations, supportive messages)
  • Breaking goals into tiny, achievable steps
  • Weekly and monthly progress celebrations
  • Positive anticipation loops to keep motivation high

The goal is long-term consistency – small actions every day, reinforced with positive emotion rather than guilt.

What It Provides to Clinicians & Clinics

For clinicians, Phase 3 creates a structured, summarised view of daily progress:

  • Weight trend & stability index
  • Behaviour consistency charts
  • Side-effect timeline
  • Medication adherence rhythm
  • Sleep–appetite correlation signals

These insights allow clinicians to make safer and faster decisions, and reduce time spent reconstructing patient history.

AI Intelligence – Elisa’s Role

Elisa is the **voice and personality** of Phase 3:

  • Understands patterns (sleep → appetite → energy → weight)
  • Explains gently: “this is normal”, “this needs attention”, “you’re improving”
  • Encourages healthy habits without pressure or fear
  • Speaks in five languages (EN, FA, AR, TR, RO)
  • Remembers context: “last week you walked more – let’s get back to that rhythm”

She is the emotional support engine of the entire weight-loss journey.

Governance, Safety & Platform Links

Phase 3 strictly follows regulatory rules:

  • Non-diagnostic – never labels or diagnoses any disease.
  • Non-prescriptive – never tells the user what dose to take.
  • Explain, not decide – clarifies patterns but leaves final actions to clinicians.
  • Zero-Trust privacy – encrypted logs, no external sharing without consent.

It also connects deeply with the rest of Elisence:

  • Phase 1: receives metabolic data (weight, GLP-1 reactions).
  • Phase 2: aligns with medication and dose history.
  • Phase 7: respects women’s hormonal and cycle factors.
  • Phase 8: feeds anonymised behaviour into risk & emergency patterns.
  • Phase 9: strengthens diabetes management through behavioural insights.
  • Phase 16: links progress to nutrition & GNIE intelligence.

Together, these create a circular ecosystem that improves every part of the user’s health.

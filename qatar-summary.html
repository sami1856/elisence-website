Elisence – Qatar Summary

A short, ministry-ready summary of Elisence for Qatar, GCC and global partners.

HealthTech AI & Digital Health EdTech & Behaviour Multi-Language National Scale

1. What Elisence Is

Elisence is a multi-phase Digital Health & AI infrastructure built across 17 phases — not just an app.

It connects GLP-1, diabetes, women’s health, mental health, nutrition, family wellbeing and national-scale dashboards into one ecosystem, fully non-diagnostic and safety-first.

2. Why It Fits Qatar & GCC

Elisence is already aligned with regional priorities:

  • Diabetes & metabolic health (Phase 9)
  • Women+ health & family wellbeing (Phases 7 & 15)
  • Nutrition intelligence (Phase 16)
  • Multi-language: EN / AR / FA / TR / RO
  • Safe, non-diagnostic AI with Zero-Trust security
  • Dashboards suitable for Ministries & public health teams

3. What Is Already Built

  • Backend engines + safety tests (GLP-1, Women+, Diabetes, AHE)
  • WORM audit log (Phase 4) + provenance export
  • Phase 10 Social layer (messaging, profiles, identity)
  • Website + videos + full documentation

4. What Elisence Seeks in Qatar

  • Incorporation & incubation as a Qatar-based HealthTech company
  • Pilot projects with clinics and ministries
  • Investment under HealthTech / AI / Digital Innovation
  • National strategy alignment (diabetes, women’s health, nutrition)

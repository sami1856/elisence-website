Founder Interviews & Insights

Questions and answers with Saami Salami about Elisence.

Sample Q&A

Why did you create Elisence?

Because I saw how fragmented digital health can be in real life — families, clinics, and systems all using different tools that don’t communicate. Elisence is my attempt to create one safe, connected ecosystem that still respects clinical boundaries and human realities.

What makes Elisence different from a normal health app?

Elisence is not “an app”. It is a multi-phase platform with governance, safety, and interoperability at the core. Each phase can be enabled or kept off until the right clinical and regulatory frameworks are in place.

How do you balance AI with human care?

AI is useful for structure, pattern recognition, and gentle coaching. It is not a replacement for doctors, nurses, or emergency services. Elisence keeps that line clear — the platform is supportive, not prescriptive.

Saami (Sami) Salami

Founder & Architect of the Elisence Global Health Intelligence Ecosystem

Also known in some records as Sami Salami

Short Biography

Saami Salami — also written in some records as Sami Salami — is the founder and chief architect of Elisence, a multi-phase global health intelligence platform designed for families, clinics, ministries of health, and regulators.

His work focuses on building safe, non-diagnostic, evidence-grade digital health systems with Zero-Trust security, WORM auditing, multi-language support (EN/FA/AR/TR/RO), and clear separation between wellness coaching and clinical decision-making.

Focus Areas

Across the Elisence roadmap, his main focus areas include:

  • Women+ Health & Life Companion — cycle, pregnancy, postpartum, and pain intelligence (Phase 7).
  • Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence — safe support around GLP-1 medications, lifestyle, and long-term risk (Phase 9).
  • Family Ecosystem 0–18 — children’s growth, sleep, digital safety, and family bonding (Phase 15).
  • Global Nutrition Intelligence (GNIE) — culturally-aware food and diet intelligence (Phase 16).
  • Mental Health & Calm — gentle, non-diagnostic emotional support modules.

Principles

Every phase of Elisence is designed under a small set of strict principles:

  • Non-diagnostic, non-prescriptive — Elisence never replaces doctors.
  • Evidence and audit first — WORM logs, provenance, and clear explanation of how each result is produced.
  • Zero-Trust security — least-privilege access, encrypted data flows, and strong governance.
  • Global-ready from day one — built for NHS, EU, GCC, and US style regulation.

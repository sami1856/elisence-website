Professional Bio — Saami Salami

Founder & Architect of Elisence

Short Overview

Saami Salami is a founder and system architect working at the intersection of digital health, clinical governance, and human-centred design. He is the creator of Elisence, a multi-phase health intelligence platform built to support families, clinics, and ministries without crossing clinical safety boundaries.

Background & Focus

With legal training and years of experience navigating complex health and regulatory environments, Saami focuses on:

  • Building safe, non-diagnostic health tools that respect clinical roles
  • Designing Zero-Trust, privacy-aware architectures for health data
  • Creating multi-language, culturally-aware health experiences
  • Bridging the gap between real families and high-level policy

Key Work — Elisence

Elisence is structured into multiple phases: Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1, Family Ecosystem 0–18, Nutrition Intelligence, Mental Health & Calm, and more — all running on top of a strong infrastructure layer with security, audit, and governance built in.

Approach & Values

Saami’s work is guided by three principles:

  • Growth — helping individuals and systems progress step by step
  • Health — focusing on practical, everyday support
  • Humanity — respecting the emotional and social reality of people’s lives

Short Founder Biography (Press-Ready)

Saami Salami Asl is the creator and architect of the Elisence Global Health Intelligence Ecosystem, a large-scale platform designed for families, clinicians, ministries, and national healthcare systems.

Coming from a legal background and lived experience navigating system failures, Saami built Elisence with one guiding principle: health intelligence must be safe, transparent, non-diagnostic, and humanity-first.

Elisence integrates Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1 logic, Family 0–18 ecosystem, Mental Health, Global Nutrition Intelligence, Zero-Trust security, provenance, WORM audit, public profiles, and messaging — into one unified, regulator-ready architecture.

What Is Elisence?

Elisence is a structured, multi-phase ecosystem combining global digital health, lifestyle intelligence, safety engines, and ministerial governance support.

  • Women+ Health Companion (Phase 7)
  • Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence (Phase 9)
  • Family Ecosystem 0–18 (Phase 15)
  • Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence (Phase 16, GNIE)
  • Mental Health & CalmNow (Phase 17)
  • Zero-Trust, Passport Engine, Provenance & WORM (Phase 4 & 8)
  • Messaging, Public Profiles, Attachments Vault (Phase 10)

Elisence is non-diagnostic, regulator-aware, privacy-first, and designed for global health systems.

Short Founder Biography (Press Use)

Saami Salami Asl is the founder and chief architect of Elisence, a multi-phase health intelligence ecosystem built to support women, families, chronic conditions, and national healthcare systems. Combining legal awareness, technology, and real-world experience of system failures, he designed Elisence to be transparent, non-diagnostic, regulator-aware, and family-centred.

Under his leadership, Elisence brings together Women+ health, diabetes and GLP-1 intelligence, family 0–18 wellbeing, mental health support, Zero-Trust security, and global nutrition intelligence into one coherent, privacy-first platform.

What Is Elisence?

Elisence is a multi-phase ecosystem that combines:

  • Women+ Health & Life Companion (Phase 7)
  • Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence (Phase 9)
  • Family Ecosystem 0–18 (Phase 15)
  • Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence (Phase 16)
  • Mental Health & CalmNow (Phase 17)
  • Zero-Trust security, Passport, WORM audit and Provenance engines (Phase 4 and 8)
  • Messaging, profiles, and social health layer (Phase 10)

The platform is explicitly non-diagnostic. It provides structured support, pattern explanations, and safe guidance, while leaving final decisions and diagnoses firmly in the hands of licensed professionals and official health systems.

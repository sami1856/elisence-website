Short Biography (Media-Ready)

Saami Salami — also known in some sources as Sami Salami — is the founder and architect of Elisence, a multi-phase global health intelligence platform designed for families, clinics, and ministries of health.

His work combines non-diagnostic digital health, Zero-Trust security, WORM-style audit logging, and multi-language UX to create systems that are safe for everyday users yet strong enough to be reviewed by regulators and health authorities.

Elisence covers Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1, Family Ecosystem 0–18, Global Nutrition Intelligence (GNIE), Mental Health & Calm, and more — all with clear boundaries between wellness support and clinical decision-making.

A more detailed biography is available on the Professional Bio page.

About Elisence

Elisence is a global health intelligence ecosystem that turns complex health and lifestyle data into safe, understandable insights for families and clinicians — without claiming to diagnose, treat, or replace medical professionals.

  • Phase 7 — Women+ Health & Life Companion
  • Phase 9 — Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence
  • Phase 10 — Messaging, Attachments, and Public Profiles
  • Phase 15 — Family Ecosystem Assistant (0–18)
  • Phase 16 — Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence Engine (GNIE)

Core technology pillars include Zero-Trust security, AKAC-style privacy supervision, WORM audit logs, provenance manifests, and clear separation between user-facing coaching and clinician-facing analytics.

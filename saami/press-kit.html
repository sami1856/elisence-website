Short Bio

Saami Salami is the founder of Elisence, a multi-phase digital health platform designed to support families, clinics, and ministries with safe, non-diagnostic health intelligence across multiple languages.

About Elisence

Elisence connects different health domains — from Women+ and Diabetes to Family 0–18 and Mental Health — to a shared, privacy-first infrastructure with Zero-Trust, auditability, and clear safety boundaries.

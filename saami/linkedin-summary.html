LinkedIn “About” Summary (Copy-Paste Ready)

I am Saami Salami Asl, Founder and Architect of Elisence — a global health platform designed to support prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, privacy, safety, and more human-centred digital health experiences.
Elisence is built around a simple belief: health technology should help people feel safer, calmer, more informed, and better supported without replacing clinical judgement.
The platform is designed around core principles including:
• Trusted health intelligence
• Prevention and continuity of care
• Human-centred digital health experiences
• Privacy-first and Zero-Trust foundations
• Multi-language accessibility and inclusion
• Responsible and explainable health support
Elisence is intended to support people, families, clinicians, organisations, and healthcare systems through connected and respectful health experiences.
Areas of focus include:
• Women+ health and wellbeing
• Metabolic and diabetes-related support
• Family health and continuity of care
• Nutrition and lifestyle intelligence
• Mental wellbeing and calm support
• Human-centred digital health experiences
My work focuses on the intersection of:
• Digital health and health platform design
• Privacy, governance, and trusted systems
• Prevention and continuity of care
• Human-centred digital health
• Long-term health ecosystem thinking
I welcome conversations with:
• Ministries of Health and public health organisations
• Hospitals, clinics, universities, and research groups
• Strategic collaborators in digital health, prevention, trusted health intelligence, and wellbeing
If you are interested in collaboration, partnerships, or long-term health innovation, I would be pleased to connect through Elisence.

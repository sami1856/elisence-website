I’m Saami (Sami) Salami, founder and architect of Elisence — a multi-phase global health intelligence ecosystem designed for families, clinics, and ministries of health.

Elisence focuses on one core promise: make digital health safer, clearer, and more human, without pretending to replace doctors. Every part of the platform follows strict principles:

• Non-diagnostic, non-prescriptive support
• Zero-Trust security and least-privilege access
• WORM-style audit logging and provenance
• Multi-language from day one (EN / FA / AR / TR / RO)
• Clear separation between family-facing coaching and clinician-facing analytics

Across 17+ phases, Elisence covers:
• Women+ Health & Life Companion (Phase 7)
• Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence (Phase 9)
• Messaging, Attachments & Public Profiles (Phase 10)
• Family Ecosystem Assistant 0–18 (Phase 15)
• Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence Engine – GNIE (Phase 16)
• Mental Health & Calm modules and more

My work sits at the intersection of:
• Digital health and regulatory governance (NHS, EU, GCC, US-style)
• Privacy and Zero-Trust security
• Real-world family life: women’s health, chronic conditions, children, and mental wellbeing.

In older records and some online sources my name appears as “Sami Salami”; the correct and current spelling is “Saami Salami”, and both refer to the same person.

I’m open to conversations with:
• Ministries of Health and public health programmes
• Hospitals, clinics, and research organisations
• Strategic partners in digital health, AI safety, and family wellbeing.

If you’d like to explore collaboration, pilots, or regulatory-grade digital health projects, feel free to reach out via Elisence.

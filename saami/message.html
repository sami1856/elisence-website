A Message from Saami

Why Elisence exists — in simple words.

To Anyone Reading This

Elisence was not created in a lab. It was born from real experiences: legal battles, health worries, family responsibilities, and the feeling that digital tools were not truly built for the people who need them most.

I wanted to build something that respects how complicated real life is — especially for women, families, and people living with long-term conditions.

What I Want Elisence to Be

I don’t want Elisence to be just another app on a crowded phone screen. I want it to be a trusted companion and a serious platform — something that doctors, ministries, and families can all look at and say: “This is careful. This is honest. This is trying to help, not just to grow numbers.”

For Families, Clinicians, and Ministries

If you are a parent, I hope Elisence can make things a little clearer and less lonely.

If you are a clinician, I hope it can help you see patterns and timelines more easily, without crossing clinical lines or adding noise.

If you are part of a ministry or public health organisation, I hope Elisence can give you safer, cleaner signals — so that planning and prevention can be based on real, anonymised trends, not guesswork.

Moving Forward

Elisence is still growing. There will be mistakes and lessons along the way. But the core promise stays the same: to build something that respects people, protects their data, and supports health in a human way.

— Saami Salami

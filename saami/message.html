Message from Saami (Sami) Salami

Founder of the Elisence Global Health Intelligence Ecosystem

To Families, Clinics, and Future Partners

Thank you for taking the time to learn about Elisence. This platform was created with one purpose: to make health information safer, clearer, and more human — for every family, every clinic, and every country.

Over the past years, I experienced how easily information can be misunderstood, how digital systems can fail people, and how quickly trust can be damaged. These experiences shaped the foundation of Elisence. I wanted a system where safety, transparency, and honesty come first — not marketing.

Elisence is built step-by-step with strict rules: Zero-Trust security, non-diagnostic boundaries, WORM auditing, multi-language support, and clear privacy controls for families and clinics.

What we are building is bigger than an app. It is a global health intelligence ecosystem that protects people’s dignity, data, and family life — while offering modern tools for ministries, clinics, and researchers.

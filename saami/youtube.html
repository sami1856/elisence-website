Core Video Themes

What Is Elisence? — Founder Explainer
A high-level overview of the Elisence global health intelligence ecosystem: why it was created, how it protects families and clinics, and how the 17-phase architecture fits together (Women+, Diabetes, Families, Nutrition, Mental Health, Security, Governance, and more).
Women+ Health — Phase 7 Overview
A non-diagnostic educational explainer on cycle health, pregnancy, postpartum, severe pain signals, and when to escalate to real-world care. The video shows how Elisence supports women with gentle guidance, pattern explanations, and safety layers — without replacing doctors or clinics.
Family Ecosystem 0–18 — Phase 15
How Elisence supports children’s growth, sleep, school life, digital safety, and emotional health. The explainer focuses on family dashboards, early pattern detection, and aggregated insights for ministries — never exposing individual child records.
Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence — Phase 9
Breaking down GLP-1 treatments, lifetime risk, adherence patterns, and safe lifestyle coaching. The focus is on education, questions to ask clinicians, and how Elisence keeps the system non-diagnostic while still providing meaningful, structured insights.
Why Zero-Trust Matters in Digital Health
Explaining how Zero-Trust security, Passport-based access, WORM audit logs, and provenance protect users, clinics, ministries, and national healthcare systems — and why Elisence was built from day one with this level of safety and evidence in mind.

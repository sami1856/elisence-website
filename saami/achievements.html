Awards & Achievements

Selected milestones from Saami (Sami) Salami’s work on Elisence

Summary

This page highlights key achievements of Saami (Sami) Salami in designing, building, and positioning the Elisence global health intelligence ecosystem — from technical breakthroughs to regulatory-grade documentation and public launches.

Design of the 17-Phase Elisence Roadmap
Architecture · 2023–2024
Created a structured, 17-phase roadmap covering core infrastructure, Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1, Provenance & WORM audit, Messaging & Public Profiles, Family 0–18, Global Nutrition Intelligence (GNIE), Mental Health & Calm, and more — each with its own safety rules, evidence model, and regulatory posture.
Regulatory-Grade Women+ Engine (Phase 7)
Women+ Health · 2024
Led the design and implementation of a Women+ Health engine that is strictly non-diagnostic, fully traceable, and aligned with NHS-style governance, including cycle, pregnancy, postpartum, and pain pathways with clear safety guards.
Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence (Phase 9)
Chronic Conditions · 2024
Developed a structured engine for GLP-1 and diabetes support with clear boundaries between lifestyle coaching and clinical care, including charting, risk summaries, and long-term evidence tracking for ministries and clinics.
Zero-Trust Security & WORM Audit Foundations
Security & Governance · 2024
Implemented core security patterns such as Zero-Trust access control, WORM-style audit logging, provenance manifests, and token-based passports, ensuring that Elisence can be reviewed by regulators, auditors, and ministries of health.
Public Launch of elisence.com
Public Website · 2024–2025
Designed and launched the public Elisence website, including phase pages, founder materials, articles, videos, and a clear positioning for families, clinics, and health authorities in multiple regions.
Family Ecosystem Assistant (0–18) Design
Phase 15 · 2025
Defined the blueprint for the Family Ecosystem Assistant, a 0–18 companion that connects growth, sleep, school, digital safety, and emotional health — with aggregated insights for schools and ministries, without exposing individual children’s records.
Global Nutrition Intelligence Engine (GNIE)
Phase 16 · 2025
Created the design for GNIE, the Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence Engine, which turns everyday food decisions into safe, culturally-aware guidance for people with diabetes, heart, kidney, liver conditions, women’s health contexts, and families.
Resilience Under Legal & Personal Pressure
Personal · 2020–2024
Continued building Elisence throughout complex legal proceedings and police allegations. Allegations were investigated and closed with no further action, while the Elisence roadmap, engines, and governance model were strengthened instead of abandoned.

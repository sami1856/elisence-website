Phase 2 · Medication & Dose Intelligence
Structured Medication Journeys
Turning GLP-1 and related prescriptions into a clear, safe and auditable story.

Phase 2 builds on the GLP-1 foundations of Phase 1 and focuses on the **medication journey itself** — which drug, which dose, when it changed, how the user responded, and what this means for safety and follow-up. It keeps a non-diagnostic posture while giving ministries, hospitals and families a transparent view of treatment over time.

Safe · Structured · Audit-Ready · Non-Diagnostic

What Phase 2 Does

Phase 2 captures and organises every important detail of the medication path for weight-loss and metabolic care:

  • Current and past GLP-1 / related drugs
  • Start and stop dates for each medication
  • Dose schedules and dose-change history
  • Simple markers of benefit, side-effects and tolerance
  • Structured notes that can later feed clinical dashboards

Who Uses It & Why

Phase 2 is designed for:

  • Individuals & families who want a clear record of which dose they were on and when.
  • Clinics & hospitals that need a clean view of the dosing journey before adjusting treatment.
  • Ministries & payers who require transparent, de-identified data for quality and safety programmes.

It never replaces doctors or clinical decisions – it simply makes the medication story **visible, consistent and auditable**.

Saami Salami Asl — Founder of Elisence

Engineer, lawyer, builder, father — creating a global digital health platform designed around trust, humanity, and long-term impact.

The Beginning

Before building Elisence, I studied Electrical Engineering. That foundation shaped the way I think: structured, logical, and based on systems that must work under pressure.

Later, I became a lawyer — and learned how to navigate complexity, evidence, truth, institutions, and the importance of protecting people.

These two worlds — engineering and law — became the blueprint for Elisence: a platform that must be technically strong, human-centered, and legally clear.

The Human Story

Elisence wasn’t born in a boardroom. It was born in real life — through love, pain, distance, resilience, and responsibility.

As a father, I wanted to build a world where people — especially children — have tools that guide them safely through life. Tools that teach discipline, thinking, emotional strength, and health.

Elisence became that world.

The Vision

Elisence is a 17-phase global platform designed to connect:

  • Families
  • Clinicians
  • Hospitals & Ministries
  • Researchers & Partners

It is built on Zero-Trust security, WORM logging, health intelligence, and non-diagnostic AI that respects human boundaries.

Why I Continue Building

Because millions of people deserve better health guidance. Because families deserve tools that don’t manipulate them. Because governments deserve trusted, honest, auditable health intelligence. Because children deserve a world designed for their wellbeing, not for addiction.

And because my daughter deserves to grow up in a world where technology loves humanity — not replaces it.

In One Sentence

Elisence is my life’s work — a platform built from heart, science, engineering, and a father’s promise.

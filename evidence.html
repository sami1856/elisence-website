B3 — Ministry Compatibility Evidence (Summary)

Ministry compatibility is not only about features – it is about safety, regulations and alignment with Qatar and GCC priorities.

  • Non-diagnostic posture: Elisence does not replace doctors; it structures data, flags risk and guides citizens to care.
  • Ministry & clinic usage: Designed for use by ministries, public hospitals, clinics and schools, not just consumer app stores.
  • Data sovereignty: Architecture aligned with GCC data-sovereignty and privacy expectations (Qatar, KSA, UAE, etc.).
  • Five-language backbone: EN / FA / AR / TR / RO – ready for GCC + wider international deployment.
  • Governance & safety: Zero-Trust, consent-first, WORM audit logs, privacy-by-design.

A deeper ministry-focused Evidence Pack (B3) can be shared privately – including policy mappings, data-flow diagrams, and governance documentation for ministries and regulators in Qatar and the GCC.

Product Evidence – Videos, UI & Storyboards

This page summarises the product-side evidence for Elisence: live videos, live website UI, and private mockups and storyboards that can be shared with ministries, clinics and investors on request.

We keep the public website clean and lightweight. Instead of filling it with screenshots and heavy assets, we use the live site and the dedicated Videos page as the primary proof of the product – and provide deeper product packs privately when needed.

5. Product Videos (Live on the Website)

Elisence already has a dedicated Videos page on the live website. Each major phase (for example Phases 4, 7, 8, 9, 10, 15, 16) has – or will have – its own short video, hosted on YouTube and embedded via this page.

  • The videos show real screens, real logic and real journeys, not concept art.
  • Each video is tightly aligned with the technical reality described on the Technical Evidence page.
  • New videos can be added without changing this Product Evidence structure.

For ministries and investors, the Videos page is the primary place to see working demos without needing any special access or logins.

Open the Videos Page

6. Live Website & Product Screens

The live Elisence website itself is a key part of the product evidence. It already exposes:

  • The overall story and positioning of Elisence.
  • The multi-phase roadmap and status (Phases 1–17).
  • The Governments hub – including dedicated pages such as Qatar.
  • The Downloads page – with public PDFs such as the Qatar summary pack.
  • The Elisa chat widget, demonstrating the companion on the public site.

For day-to-day evaluation, the live website is stronger evidence than screenshots: it shows that the product is already coherent, navigable and ready to present to ministries and clinics.

We deliberately do not fill this page with static screenshots. Instead:

  • The live website is the primary “screen evidence”.
  • For offline or internal reviews (for example, inside a ministry, QDB or a clinic), we can provide a curated set of high-resolution screenshots inside a PDF evidence pack.

High-resolution screenshots of key flows (home, phases, governments/Qatar, downloads, videos, etc.) are available on request as part of a private product evidence pack.

7. UI Mockups (Ministry, Clinic & Citizen Views)

In addition to the live website and videos, Elisence maintains a set of high-fidelity UI mockups for:

  • Ministry and public-health dashboards.
  • Clinician panels (AHE, labs, diabetes, women+ signals).
  • Citizen and family apps (weight, diabetes, women+, mental health, family ecosystem).

These mockups are used in design reviews, UX workshops and closed evaluation sessions with ministries, clinics and serious investors.

To keep the public website lightweight and controlled, we do not publish all mockups publicly. Instead, they can be shared:

  • Privately during evaluation sessions (for example with a ministry team or QDB Digital Health unit).
  • Under NDA or as part of a structured due-diligence process.

Detailed UI mockups for ministry dashboards, clinician panels and citizen/family views are therefore part of the private Product Evidence Pack, not the public website.

8. Storyboards for Key Phases

For key phases – such as Phase 4 (ECI / WORM / Provenance), Phase 7 (Women+), Phase 8 (AHE), Phase 9 (Diabetes), Phase 10 (Social Identity / Messaging), Phase 15 (Family Ecosystem) and Phase 16 (Global Nutrition Engine) – Elisence has structured storyboards that describe:

  • What a citizen, clinician or ministry user sees on each screen.
  • Which questions they ask and which answers Elisence provides.
  • How safety, non-diagnostic boundaries and consent are communicated.
  • How the experience unfolds over time (for example: first visit, follow-up, escalation).

These storyboards are the bridge between engines and real experiences: they inform the videos on the Videos page and guide the design of future UI components.

Full storyboard documents (scene-by-scene) are not published on the public site. Instead, they are available as part of the internal Product Evidence Pack and can be shared with ministries, clinics or investors when needed.

Summary – Public vs Private Product Evidence

Not every internal asset (videos, screenshots, mockups, storyboards) is published publicly. This is intentional: it keeps the public-facing surface of Elisence clean, fast and focused, while still allowing deep evaluation when required.

  • Public: live website, Governments/Qatar pages, Downloads (high-level PDFs), Videos page, Elisa widget.
  • Private on request: curated screenshots, high-fidelity mockups, detailed storyboards and combined Product Evidence PDFs.

For ministries, regulators, clinics or investors who need a deeper Product Evidence Pack, we can share private materials (for example under NDA or as part of QDB/QFC/QSTP or ministry evaluations). The contact point is the same as for Technical Evidence: use the contact page or email contact@elisence.com.

B3 — Ministry Compatibility Evidence (Qatar & GCC Ready)

Elisence is designed as a national-grade digital health intelligence platform, within a strict non-diagnostic boundary, aligned to governance-grade evidence expectations.

  • Non-diagnostic / non-prescriptive posture: no diagnoses, no prescriptions, no drug dosing advice.
  • Data sovereignty: deployable inside-country or approved regions with clear separation of tiers.
  • Zero-Trust: strict passports/headers, least privilege, consent-first access.
  • WORM audit: append-only logs for regulator-grade traceability.
  • Five-language baseline: EN / FA / AR / TR / RO with RTL readiness.
  • BBVP: Build–Back–Verify–Prove evidence discipline phase by phase.

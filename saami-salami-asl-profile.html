Saami Salami Asl

Founder & Architect of Elisence — Global Health Platform

Short Founder Profile

Saami Salami Asl is the Founder and Architect of Elisence, a global health platform focused on prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, privacy, safety, and human-centred digital health.

Elisence is designed to help healthcare feel more connected, preventative, supportive, trusted, and easier to navigate over time.

Founder Work & Vision

Saami Salami Asl leads the long-term founder vision behind Elisence, including prevention, trusted health participation, continuity-focused digital health, and privacy-aware health technology.

  • Women+ health and wellbeing
  • Metabolic and diabetes-related support
  • Family health and continuity of care
  • Trusted health intelligence
  • Nutrition and lifestyle intelligence
  • Privacy-first, governance-aware platform design

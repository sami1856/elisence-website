02 — MEET ELISENCE

Meet Elisence

One Connected AI-Powered Digital Health Ecosystem

It connects prevention, nutrition, chronic disease management, medication, GP services, laboratory intelligence, social health, family engagement and continuous AI-powered guidance into one seamless health journey.

03 — WHY COMPENSAR + ELISENCE

Why Compensar + Elisence?

Turning healthy nutrition into a connected, measurable and long-term digital health journey.

Compensar’s challenge is not only to help members eat better. The real opportunity is to keep people engaged over time, support chronic conditions, motivate families, connect daily behaviour with health intelligence, and create measurable outcomes that can scale from pilot to population-level impact.

TODAY'S MEMBER EXPERIENCE
  • Food tracking often stops after the first few weeks.
  • Healthy habits are difficult to maintain without daily motivation.
  • Chronic patients receive fragmented support across apps, clinics and home life.
  • Family members are rarely part of the behaviour-change journey.
  • Healthcare teams have limited visibility between appointments.

What Is Elisence?

Elisence is a global digital health & humanity platform — a unified ecosystem designed to make health guidance safer, more intelligent, culturally aware, and truly human. Built on government-grade safety architecture, it delivers non-diagnostic, multilingual, high-integrity intelligence for families, clinicians, ministries and public-health organisations.

Founder — Saami Salami Asl

Elisence was founded by Saami Salami Asl, a British lawyer and former electrical engineer with deep experience working with patients, carers, clinics and hospitals. His mission is to build the world’s safest and most human-centred digital health ecosystem — AI that is truthful, kind, culturally aware, and aligned with real-life progress.

Mission

To deliver a global digital health platform that respects dignity, protects safety, avoids addictive design, and supports every user with culturally-aware guidance — across 5 languages (English, Persian, Arabic, Turkish and Romanian).

What Elisence Provides

  • Non-diagnostic, government-grade health intelligence

Safety & Integrity

Safety is the foundation of Elisence. The platform uses a non-diagnostic, non-prescriptive model with:

  • Government-grade safety gates
  • Zero-Trust architecture
  • WORM audit logging + SHA-256 integrity
  • NHS, EU MDR, GCC PDPL alignment
  • Full multilingual tone adaptation (EN/FA/AR/TR/RO)

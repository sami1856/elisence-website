02 — Problem

Healthcare Feels Fragmented

Fragmentation, rising costs, and growing system pressure

Medication Women’s Health GP / Doctor Pharmacy Metabolism Body Tracking Wellbeing Prevention

For People

  • Health journeys are fragmented across disconnected apps, appointments, medications, trackers, and systems.
  • Many people manage diabetes, prevention, chronic conditions, wellbeing, family care, and medication across multiple tools — often with little continuity.
  • Health can feel confusing, stressful, isolating, expensive, and difficult to navigate.

For Healthcare Systems

  • Growing pressure from chronic disease, ageing populations, workforce strain, fragmented care journeys, and rising costs.
  • Prevention, continuity, trusted participation, and long-term engagement are increasingly important.
  • Better coordination and continuity may help reduce inefficiency, duplication, and avoidable burden over time.

Healthcare should feel more connected, understandable, affordable, and human.

03 — Why Now

A Convergence Moment

Healthcare is reaching a turning point

Healthcare pressure

Chronic conditions rising, populations ageing, costs increasing — systems must move from reactive care toward prevention and continuity.

People managing more health

Medication, diabetes, prevention, wellbeing, family care, and everyday decisions are pushed onto individuals — yet experiences stay fragmented.

Digital expectations changed

People expect connected, intelligent experiences in life — but healthcare still often feels disconnected, episodic, and hard to coordinate.

Technology maturity

Digital infrastructure, richer health signals, sensor-aware experiences, AI-assisted workflows, and secure communication now enable long-term engagement in a trusted way.

Reactive → Preventative  |  Episodic → Continuous  |  Fragmented → Connected

Healthcare pressure, behaviour change, technology maturity, and trust are finally converging.

04 — What is Elisence

One Connected Ecosystem

A connected health ecosystem for people and healthcare systems

Elisence is a human-centred, globally adaptable health ecosystem designed to make healthcare feel more connected, understandable, and easier to navigate over time.

For People

  • Prevention, diabetes and metabolic health, medical tracking, medication continuity, intelligence, body understanding, women’s health, family care, trusted guidance, and care-provider communication — in one connected experience.

One connected ecosystem — human, trusted, continuous.

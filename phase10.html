PHASE 10

User Profiles, Social Presence & Secure Attachments

PROFILES · SOCIAL GRAPH · ATTACHMENTS VAULT · SAFETY-FIRST

Phase 10 is the public face and social heart of Elisence. It turns people, clinicians, clinics and ministries from silent data rows into living profiles, conversations and communities – under a strict Zero-Trust, evidence-ready architecture.

  • The vitrine where all other phases become visible and human.
  • Rich profiles, social presence and a healthcare-grade Attachments & Media Vault.
  • Designed as a safe alternative to generic social networks for health.

1. What Phase 10 Really Is

Phase 10 is Elisence’s User Profile & Social Identity Layer. It is where identities, stories and achievements live:

  • Rich personal profiles for citizens and families.
  • Verified professional profiles for doctors, clinics, pharmacists and partners.
  • Social presence: posts, reactions, shared achievements and topic communities.
  • A secure Attachments & Media Vault for documents, scans, reports and legal files.
  • Discovery and interaction that are safer than mainstream social networks, because they are built for health, not clicks.

Design principles:

  • Zero-Trust by design and BBVP (Build • Back • Verify • Prove) for every module.
  • NHS / GDPR / EU MDR / HIPAA / GCC PDPL aware, with clear non-diagnostic boundaries.
  • Five-language foundation from day one: EN, FA, AR, TR, RO.

2. Citizen & Family Profiles

2.1 Personal Identity

Phase 10 gives every user a safe, modern health identity. A profile can include a photo or avatar, a short bio, and key health focuses such as weight-loss, diabetes support, Women+ health, or mental wellbeing. Every field is under the user’s control.

Privacy levels are simple and explicit: private, friends-only or public. Nothing is exposed by accident.

2.2 Family & Care Profiles

Parents and carers can manage profiles for children or elderly relatives, linked to the Elisence Care modules. All sharing is consent-based and fully traceable. Sensitive details never leak into public views.

2.3 Healthy Social Feed (Not Addictive)

Users can post progress updates, celebrate milestones from their weight-loss, diabetes or Women+ journeys, and share positive achievements powered by the Healthy Dopamine engine. The algorithm is explicitly not addictive:

  • No casino-style loops, no dark patterns, no engagement traps.
  • Only reinforcement of healthy, sustainable behaviours.
  • No shaming, no comparison leaderboards, no toxic metrics.

3. Professional & Organisation Profiles

3.1 Verified Professional Identity

Phase 10 allows doctors, nurses, dietitians, psychologists, pharmacists, clinics and hospitals to hold verified professional profiles. Verification is tied to Elisence Passport V1 and the Advanced Health Engine (Phase 8), with national rules for who can be approved.

Professional profiles can show:

  • Credentials, specialities and professional affiliations.
  • Opening hours, clinic locations and accepted programmes.
  • Educational posts, community Q&A and public updates.

3.2 Rules Defined by Ministries & Regulators

Ministries and regulators configure:

  • Which roles can be verified and under what process.
  • What professional accounts are allowed to publish.
  • Which regions, insurers and public programmes are enabled first.

The result is a trustable health network, not a free-for-all forum.

4. Secure Attachments & Media Vault

4.1 One Safe Place for Health Documents

The Attachments & Media Vault is one of the core innovations of Phase 10. Users can store and share:

  • Lab results, imaging, clinic and hospital letters.
  • Prescription photos and medication summaries.
  • Legal and consent documents.
  • Photos and videos relevant to their health journey.

4.2 Access Control & WORM Audit

Access is fully user-controlled: they decide who can see each file, for how long, and for what purpose.

  • Time-limited secure links and revocation controls.
  • WORM audit logs for all critical actions and sharing events.
  • Fine-grained roles for family, clinicians, hospitals and ministries.

Elisence does not rely on random public file links or consumer clouds. The entire flow runs inside the Elisence infrastructure with healthcare-grade security and passport-based access control.

5. Safer Social Interaction

5.1 Built for Health, Not for Clicks

Phase 10 includes familiar social features – posts, comments, reactions and topic spaces – but rebuilt for health contexts:

  • Clear content rules and moderation playbooks.
  • Zero tolerance for abuse, hate or organised health misinformation.
  • Reporting, escalation and bans for harmful behaviour.

Social interactions happen in a container designed for care, not for ad impressions or engagement metrics.

6. Voice, Accessibility & Languages

6.1 Voice-First Social Experience

With Phase 13 (Voice Engine), users can browse profiles, open posts, listen to updates and send short messages entirely by voice. Elisa can read key updates aloud and help visually-impaired users navigate the social layer safely.

6.2 Accessibility & Blind-Assist

Phase 10 integrates with the Blind/Accessibility modules:

  • High-contrast UI and clear typography.
  • Voice-first flows and captioned content.
  • Camera-assisted descriptions that allow visually impaired users to participate fully in the community.

6.3 Five-Language Foundation

All profile labels, system messages and structural text are designed to support five languages from day one: English, Farsi, Arabic, Turkish and Romanian – with a clear path to add more in future, including full RTL support where needed.

7. Integration With Other Phases — The Vitrine of Elisence

Phase 10 is where other phases become visible and human:

  • Phase 3 & 9: weight, GLP-1 and diabetes milestones become shareable achievements (if the user chooses).
  • Phase 5: Healthy Dopamine events appear as subtle, ethical rewards in the social feed.
  • Phase 6: Structured medication behaviour can drive safe, anonymised badges and streaks.
  • Phase 7: Women+ journeys connect to respectful, trauma-aware communities.
  • Phase 8: Professional and institutional identities are anchored via Passport V1 and AHE.

In practice, Phase 10 is the vitrine where citizens, families, clinicians and ministries see the living side of Elisence.

8. Regulatory & Government Posture

8.1 Safe by Default

Citizen-facing flows are strictly non-diagnostic and non-prescriptive. Medical-adjacent features (professional dashboards, clinical pathways, public programmes) are guarded behind ON/OFF feature flags and can only be enabled by ministries or accredited health systems.

Phase 10 is designed to align with:

  • NHS DTAC / DCB0129–0160 principles.
  • GDPR / UK DPA.
  • EU MDR boundaries for non-device software.
  • HIPAA-style expectations for health data.
  • GCC / Qatar / Saudi PDPL data-residency and privacy rules.

Every critical action can be logged to WORM audit trails. BBVP is applied to every module so that new features are always shipped with evidence packs and tests, not just promises.

9. Why Phase 10 Matters

For users, Phase 10 is where Elisence feels alive: a place to belong, to be seen, to receive support and to share progress safely.

For clinicians and hospitals, Phase 10 is a trusted social–clinical bridge, combining professional identity, secure documents and patient-friendly communication under clear rules.

For ministries and investors, Phase 10 is a strategic moat: it owns the health-centric social graph, provides a safe alternative to generic social networks for health discussions, and binds all other Elisence phases into one coherent public-facing experience.

10. One-Sentence Summary

Phase 10 turns Elisence from a powerful health engine into a trusted, human social universe – where profiles, conversations, achievements and secure documents live together under one globally compliant, Zero-Trust architecture.

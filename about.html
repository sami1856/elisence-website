About · Elisence Platform

Elisence — Sovereign Digital Health & Humanity Platform

Elisence is a global, sovereign digital health infrastructure designed and founded by Saami Salami Asl (also written as Sami Salami Asl or Saami Salami). It is a 17-phase, multilingual health intelligence ecosystem built on Zero-Trust AI, non-diagnostic behavioral models, and national-grade data governance.

Mission

1. Our Mission

Elisence aims to create the world’s safest and most intelligent health ecosystem — one that supports users, clinicians, hospitals, ministries, families, and vulnerable populations across GCC, MENA, UK, and EU.

The platform is designed as a sovereign digital health infrastructure that governments can trust and scale, with clear boundaries between wellness intelligence and regulated clinical care.

Learn more in the platform overview: Elisence – The Global Digital Health Platform.

Architecture

2. The 17-Phase Architecture

Every component of Elisence — from Women+ Health and Diabetes Intelligence to Accessibility, Mental Health, Family (0–18), Environment (Phase 12), and Global Nutrition (Phase 16) — is modular, feature-flagged, clinically aware and built for ministry-grade deployment.

  • Each phase can be turned ON/OFF via feature flags.
  • Non-diagnostic, explainable AI is used across the system.
  • Data flows are designed for WORM auditing and national governance.
Governments & Regulators

3. Why Governments Trust Elisence

Elisence has been engineered for governments, regulators and ministries who demand auditable, sovereign and culturally aware technology.

  • Zero-Trust Health AI with strict guardrails and event logging.
  • WORM-ledger audit layer for every critical interaction.
  • National data residency & sovereignty (Qatar-ready design).
  • Non-diagnostic, patient-first architecture.
  • Five-language intelligence (EN, AR, FA, TR, RO).
  • Integration-ready for Cerner, Epic and FHIR-based systems.
Founder

Global Vision

5. Our Global Vision

Elisence is designed as a universal, sovereign platform that can serve as the foundation of national digital health strategies across GCC, Europe and beyond — a platform where safety, privacy, culture and intelligence work together in one unified system.

Whether deployed for a single hospital network or at national scale, the core principle remains the same: health technology must protect humans first and stay explainable to clinicians, regulators and families.

Humanity & Families

6. Built for Real People, Not Just Systems

Behind every phase of Elisence there is a human story — parents, children, caregivers, clinicians and vulnerable people whose lives are affected by health decisions every day.

Elisence is built not only as infrastructure, but as a humanity platform: a space where Women+, Diabetes, Mental Health, Kids & Teens, Environment and Nutrition are all treated as connected parts of the same real life.

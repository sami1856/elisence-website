Saami Salami Asl

Founder & Architect of Elisence — Global Health Intelligence Platform

Founder Profile

Saami Salami Asl is the founder and architect of Elisence, a global health intelligence platform designed around trust, prevention, privacy, continuity, and human-centred digital health.

His work focuses on trusted, privacy-first digital health systems including Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1 intelligence, family health, wellbeing, nutrition intelligence, and long-term health continuity.

Elisence is designed as a connected digital health ecosystem for individuals, families, clinicians, healthcare systems, and public institutions.

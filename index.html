GLOBAL INTRO
Elisence — The Global Digital Health & Humanity Platform

High-level overview of Elisence: families, clinics, ministries, Zero-Trust security and the 17-phase architecture behind the platform.

PHASE 1
Core Monitoring & Early Signals

Foundations for safe monitoring, vital signs, red-flag detection and early escalation paths for citizens and clinics.

PHASE 2
Risk, Labs & Emergency Intelligence

Blood, risk and emergency intelligence: lab results, escalation rules and event-driven alerts for safer urgent care.

PHASE 3
Care, Weight-Loss & Lifestyle

Weight-loss and lifestyle engine for GLP-1, exercise, sleep and habits — fully non-diagnostic, coach-style support.

PHASE 4
Integrity, Provenance & WORM

Provenance engine, WORM audit logs and integrity bundles that make Elisence verifiable for ministries and regulators.

PHASE 5
Voice & Zero-Typing Engine

Conversational, voice-first access to Elisence so citizens can talk rather than type, with strict safety and governance.

PHASE 6
Comparative Analytics & Insight

Anonymised, k-anonymous analytics for ministries and hospitals — never exposing any individual’s record.

PHASE 7
Women+ Health & Life Companion

Women+ engine for cycle health, pregnancy, postpartum and safety — multilingual, traceable and non-diagnostic.

PHASE 8
Safety, AHE & Regulatory Backbone

Safety, AHE and SaMD boundaries with test harnesses, hazard logs and risk-to-control mapping for real-world review.

PHASE 9
Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence

Diabetes, metabolic and GLP-1 intelligence: long-term risk, events, lifestyle and non-diagnostic coaching.

PHASE 10
Social & Identity Layer (ELIS)

Messaging, public profiles and social identity with strict passports, attachments vault and boundary governance.

PHASE 11
Safety, AHE Tests & Evidence

Safety evidence packs, WORM-grade audit exports and alignment with NHS, EU MDR, FDA and GCC baselines.

PHASE 12
Family, Kids & Guardianship Layer

Family and kids layer: permissions, guardianship and safe collaboration around children’s health journeys.

PHASE 13
Global Care Teams & Collaboration

Multi-role care teams, shared dashboards and secure collaboration across clinics, hospitals, ministries and borders.

PHASE 14
Life Map & Daily Companion

Life Map companion: daily life, safety and meaningful moments — designed especially for vulnerable users.

PHASE 15
Family Ecosystem Assistant (0–18)

Family Ecosystem Assistant: 0–18 wellbeing, school, digital safety, bonding and aggregated insights for ministries.

PHASE 16
Global Nutrition & Diet Engine (GNIE)

Nutrition & diet intelligence that respects culture, official guidelines and real-world risks, across five languages.

PHASE 17
Mental Health & CalmNow Engine

Mental health & calm engine for safe mood support, early risk flags and gentle, non-diagnostic guidance.

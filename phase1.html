Phase 1 – GLP-1 Foundations & Safety

GLP-1 & Metabolic Intelligence Core

Phase 1 is the metabolic foundation of Elisence. It captures GLP-1 and related medication journeys in a safe, structured way, so that ministries, clinicians and families can trust every step before building more advanced engines on top.

What Phase 1 Does

Phase 1 is not a diet app or a prescription tool. It is a safety-first engine that understands how GLP-1 and similar medications are being used in real life and turns that into structured signals.

  • Captures core facts: medication name, dose, schedule and route.
  • Tracks weight trends and key metabolic context without giving diagnosis.
  • Highlights simple, non-diagnostic safety patterns that may need attention.
  • Prepares clean, auditable data for later phases such as Phase 4 and Phase 8.

Safety & Non-Diagnostic Boundaries

Phase 1 stays firmly on the wellness and safety side of the line. It does not replace a doctor, clinic or hospital, and it never tells users to change their medication.

  • No diagnosis, no prescriptions, no dose adjustments.
  • Language tuned for “things to watch” and “questions to ask your clinician”.
  • Simple traffic-light style signals that can feed into later clinical tools.
  • Designed for NHS, EU, GCC and global SaMD boundary expectations.

Who Uses Phase 1 and How It Fits the 17-Phase Roadmap

Phase 1 is designed to be useful to normal families, clinicians and ministries from day one, while also acting as the backbone for future phases of Elisence.

  • Families and individuals log simple, structured information about medication and weight.
  • Clinicians and clinics can review clear summaries instead of raw chat logs or screenshots.
  • Ministries and hospitals can later plug this core into larger analytics engines.

The outputs of Phase 1 are designed to connect directly into: Phase 3 (Care & Weight-Loss Journeys), Phase 4 (Provenance & Integrity Engine), Phase 8 (AHE & Clinical Profiles), and Phase 9 (Diabetes & Metabolic Health).

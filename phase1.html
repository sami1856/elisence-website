PHASE 1

GLP-1 & Metabolic Intelligence Core

Phase 1 is the GLP-1 and weight / metabolic intelligence foundation of Elisence – the place where real-world data about weight, medication, side effects, behaviour, and habits is transformed into safe, non-diagnostic insights for users, clinicians, and future national programmes.

What Phase 1 Does

From numbers on a scale to a clear, structured picture of metabolic health.

Phase 1 collects and organises core metabolic signals, including:

  • Weight trends and body-mass changes over time
  • GLP-1 and related medication doses (e.g. Ozempic, Mounjaro, Wegovy)
  • Side effects, benefits, and day-to-day symptoms
  • Sleep, appetite, energy patterns and lifestyle behaviours
  • Key context such as timing, adherence, and basic routines

The goal of Phase 1 is simple: build a clean, structured metabolic “timeline” for each user – something clear enough for a person to understand, and precise enough that a clinician or ministry could later use it safely as part of a wider system.

How It Works for Users

For individuals and families, Phase 1 feels like a calm, organised, GLP-1 companion rather than a spreadsheet of numbers.

  • Simple daily or weekly check-ins instead of long forms
  • Clear trends (e.g. “your weight is stabilising”, “side effects are decreasing”)
  • Gentle alerts if something looks unusual or worth discussing with a doctor
  • Support from Elisa, who translates data into plain, human language

Phase 1 never tells people what dose to take, never replaces clinical advice, and never acts as a prescriber. It simply organises and explains.

How It Supports Clinicians & Clinics

For doctors, nurses and clinics, Phase 1 is designed to become a structured “GLP-1 & metabolic snapshot” that answers questions quickly:

  • What has actually happened to weight since starting GLP-1 therapy?
  • What side effects appeared, when, and in what context?
  • How stable are the user’s routines, sleep and energy levels?
  • Is the overall pattern improving, plateauing, or concerning?

This allows clinicians to spend less time trying to reconstruct history, and more time making safe, human decisions together with the patient.

Governance & Safety Principles

Phase 1 follows the same safety philosophy as the rest of Elisence:

  • Non-diagnostic: it never labels, diagnoses, or prescribes.
  • Explain, not decide: it helps people understand patterns, not replace doctors.
  • Zero-trust security: all data is treated as sensitive by default.
  • Clinical-ready structure: the data can later feed phases 4 and 8 for national use.

The outputs of Phase 1 are designed to plug directly into future Phase 4 (Governance & Infrastructure) and Phase 8 (Advanced Health Engine) when ministries or hospitals want to scale.

Role Inside the 17-Phase Roadmap

Phase 1 is the metabolic foundation of Elisence. It connects directly to:

  • Phase 2 & 3: medication choice, daily care and weight-loss coaching
  • Phase 7: women’s health and hormone-linked metabolic changes
  • Phase 8: blood markers, risk engines, emergency-related patterns
  • Phase 9: full diabetes and long-term metabolic health
  • Phase 16: nutrition & GNIE – personal diet and environment-aware guidance

By starting from Phase 1, Elisence builds a stable, trustworthy base that can grow safely into a national-scale health intelligence and support system.

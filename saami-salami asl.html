Saami Salami Asl

Founder of Elisence — a global health platform built for prevention, trusted health intelligence, and continuity of care.

Official Founder Profile

Saami Salami Asl is the founder of Elisence, a global health platform focused on prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, privacy, safety, and human-centred digital health.

Elisence is designed to support people, families, clinicians, organisations, and health systems through calmer, safer, more connected digital health experiences.

Founder Vision

The founder vision behind Elisence is to make health feel more connected, preventative, trusted, and human. The platform is being built around long-term health participation, responsible intelligence, privacy-first design, and clear safety boundaries.

  • Prevention and continuity of care
  • Trusted health intelligence
  • Human-centred digital health
  • Privacy-first and Zero-Trust foundations
  • Family, women’s health, metabolic health, and wellbeing support

The Elisence Platform

Under the leadership of Saami Salami Asl, Elisence is being developed as a long-term global health ecosystem connecting prevention, wellbeing, medication support, metabolic health, women’s health, family care, trusted health intelligence, and continuity of care.

  • Women+ health and life companion systems
  • Metabolic health and diabetes-related support
  • Family health and wellbeing experiences
  • Nutrition intelligence and calm health support
  • Security, auditability, and governance foundations

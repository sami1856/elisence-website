Who Is Saami Salami?

Saami Salami is the founder and architect of Elisence, a global health intelligence platform designed to connect families, clinics, and ministries around safer, more transparent digital health.

With a background in law, technology, and real-world experience across different health systems, Saami focuses on building platforms that respect privacy, human dignity, and clinical realities — not just pure “apps” or short-term trends.

Elisence — The Main Work

Elisence is a multi-phase ecosystem, including:

  • Women+ Health & Life Companion (Phase 7)
  • Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence (Phase 9)
  • Family Ecosystem 0–18 (Phase 15)
  • Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence (Phase 16)
  • Mental Health & Calm Support (Phase 17)
  • Zero-Trust and governance infrastructure at the core

Who Is Saami Salami Asl?

Saami Salami Asl is the Founder and Architect of Elisence, a global health platform designed to support prevention, trusted health intelligence, continuity of care, and more connected human-centred digital health experiences.

With experience across law, technology, and real-world health system challenges, Saami Salami Asl focuses on building Elisence as a trusted health ecosystem that respects privacy, human dignity, clinical realities, family needs, and the long-term future of connected healthcare.

Elisence — The Main Work

Elisence is being developed as a multi-phase global health ecosystem designed to make healthcare feel more continuous, preventative, intelligent, and human.

  • Women+ Health & Life Companion
  • Diabetes & GLP-1 Intelligence
  • Family Health Ecosystem
  • Global Nutrition & Diet Intelligence
  • Mental Health & Calm Support
  • Trusted health intelligence and continuity of care
  • Zero-Trust governance and safety infrastructure

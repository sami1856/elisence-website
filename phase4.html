Phase 4

Phase 4 is the point where Elisence becomes evidence-grade infrastructure: stable analytics, strict integrity, and audit-ready outputs suitable for ministries, hospitals, regulators, and universities.

This layer exists to answer a single question with full transparency: Can independent reviewers trust these numbers at national scale? Phase 4 enforces verifiable integrity across the full journey: data ingestion, quality gates, analytics computation, provenance metadata, and controlled exports. Every result is non-diagnostic, privacy-safe, and designed for governance.

Phase 4 is implemented under BBVP and Zero-Trust posture, with five-language readiness (EN / FA / AR / TR / RO), evidence packs, and PDF reporting designed for institutional review.

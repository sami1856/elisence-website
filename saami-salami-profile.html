Sami (Saami) Salami

Founder of Elisence — Global Health Intelligence Ecosystem

Short Profile

Sami Salami — written in many official documents as Saami Salami — is the founder and architect of Elisence, a multi-phase global health intelligence platform designed for families, clinics, and ministries.

His work focuses on safe, non-diagnostic digital health: Women+ Health, Diabetes & GLP-1, Family Ecosystem 0–18, Nutrition Intelligence, and Mental Health & Calm, all built on a Zero-Trust, privacy-first infrastructure with strong audit and governance.
